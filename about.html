About Hitman Master

Welcome to Hitman Master, the ultimate test of stealth, strategy, and precision. Assume the role of Agent 77, an elite operative tasked with navigating complex environments and completing impossible objectives.

Travel from high-security bases to bustling city streets, using only your wits, an arsenal of gadgets, and flawless execution to remain undetected. Every decision matters. Analyze your target, blend into the shadows, and strike when the moment is perfect.

Whether you choose to disappear like a ghost or execute a tactical assault, the choices are yours. Mastering the art of elimination is a fine skill. Prepare yourself, Agent. Your mission begins now.
